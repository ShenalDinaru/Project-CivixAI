About Civix AI

What we do · Why we exist

CivixAI bridges the gap between the government and the public by transforming complex taxation processes into easy-to-follow guidance.

What is Civix AI?

CivixAI is an intelligent, user-friendly platform created to help Sri Lankan citizens understand and manage tax-related tasks with ease.

Our Vision

To create an informed, empowered, and financially responsible Sri Lankan society by making tax information accessible, understandable, and easy to act upon.

Our Mission

To close the knowledge gap between citizens and government tax systems by providing user-friendly tools that educate, guide, and support responsible tax practices.
